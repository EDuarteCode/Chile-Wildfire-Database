# Persistent Wildfire Risk in Mediterranean Chile: Climate, Land-Use, and Spatial Patterns of Fire Recurrence

Claudia Garrido-Ruiz1, Efrain Duarte2*

1 Department of Soils, Plants & Climate, Utah State University, Logan UT 84322, USA.
